The MBA Tech stack for Job hunting

Rakovski published on 2020-10-23 included in Career
Who is this guide for? For those who are targeting a fragmented market, where job hunting is similar to Sales, where you have to set up the funnel and churn through until you get a positive result. Some examples of fragmented job markets are: any VC role, BizDev, CorpDev, Sales, Marketing, Product Management, Account Management, Internal Strategy, Ops. Here, the biggest predictor of success is Volume of applications. To increase Volume, you need to make applying to jobs as pain-free as possible.
